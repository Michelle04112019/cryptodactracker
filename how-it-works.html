DAC Strategy for Long-Term Investment

The DAC strategy is a simple but effective approach to investing in cryptocurrencies over the long term. It relies on three key principles:

  • Diversification: Invest in multiple cryptocurrencies to reduce risk.
  • Accumulation: Buy regularly to benefit from cost averaging and take advantage of compounding effects.
  • Conviction: Choose strong projects that you believe in for the long term.

How to Track Your Portfolio?

Why Use Crypto DAC Tracker?

This tracker helps you monitor your portfolio in a clear and concise manner while sticking to a long-term investment strategy. It allows you to make informed decisions without falling victim to short-term market pressures.
